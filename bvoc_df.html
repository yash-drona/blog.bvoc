What is the difference between Bachelor of Design and BVoc Design?

What is the difference between Bachelor of Design and BVoc Design? In Bachelor of Design you will study in college and classroom. But in BVoc Design it has classroom study as well as you have to work on a live project in a design company or as a Intern in a designing company.

So after three years a Bachelor of Design student may be a fresher but a BVoc design student will not be a fresher, he will also have three years of design experience and therefore his employability will increase or even if he wants to do START UP himself then he will have the experience and knowledge to start one.

What is the benefit of BVoc design?

Anyone can take admission after 12th standard, you don't need to give NATA, NID, NIFT entrance exam. Many times students spend lakhs of rupees in preparation for entrance and still don't pass. You can take direct entry in this B.Voc Design is a three-year bachelor degree six semester course this bachelor degree is like other bachelor courses like BA, BSC, BCom.

If you can do really good designing from the first year then you can also do stipend. Again design is a creative business so if even if you got less in 12th we would accept you. It is always tried in B.Voc that you earn while you are studying.
