What are the Eligibility criteria for doing BVoc?

Anyone can do this degree after 12th standard in Arts, Commerce, Science. Anyone can take admission in this. It is not necessary that Commerce students can do it or Science students can do it. This degree is of three years because it is a bachelor degree then three years and 6 semesters are the same as in B.B.A., B.Com, B.Sc.

Inshort the students who have passed their 12th/HSC are eligible to pursue B.Voc and also after diploma.

Difference between BVoc and all other degrees?

  • You may be wondering why we have to do BVoc? There are degrees for all specializations like BSc Agriculture, B.Sc Organic Farming, BBA Finance, BSc Banking, Finance or B.Com, BSc Management, Bachelor of Journalism Mass, same common thing is that the traditional degrees till date were theory based. This all degree is focused on theory and bookish knowledge and BVoc is focusing on theory as well as practical knowledge. The students of BVoc have on the job training and live projects.

  • In other degrees you have to go to college for 6 hours sitting in class and studying your particular course or rather if you are pursuing B.Voc, students have to attend class only for 2 hours and have to do internships or on the job training for 6 hours in companies which are related to particular specialization.

  • If you are doing B.Voc in Organic Farming than you have to spend 60 percent of you time in organic farms .If you do B.Voc in finance then you have to spend 60 percent of your time in a Chartered Accountant or Bank DSA (Direct selling agent). If you do a B.Voc in management, then you have to spend time in any management related company: marketing, real estate, retail company.

  • Why B.Voc is important? Lets say you want to learn swimming, If someone teach you all day in the classroom PPT or by taking you to industrial visit i.e. visit swimming pool and show the swimming pool than you make a project report on swimming pool, Finally at the end of the semester you get 100 out of 100 marks in the exam, you have only knowledge swimming.

  • But if you want to learn swimming actually then you have to do swimming, if you want to swim then you have to actually jump into the swimming pool you can't learn swimming just from theory then similarly in management, finance, software development or communication, How can you do all these things, this was the big problem of our education system till now.

  • Students had only theoretical knowledge. Practicing knowledge was nothing. In the name of practical knowledge, we do project reports of industrial visits but never do actual practice. When we go for job interviews after studying, people tell us that you are a fresher, when people tell you that what you have studied is of no use to come here, something else is going on.

  • To prevent this from happening, the government has started a B.Voc degree. Because traditional courses are based on just theoretical knowledge, more than 50% of the graduates are unemployed, and most of the students are struggling with a B.B.A., B.Com or B.Sc. Now what is the point of doing business or a job doing something different because he doesn't have skills to work in the field in which he got his degree.

  • That knowledge has no meaning, B.Voc has been started to solve this problem, when you do Bvoc you have to do internship/job from the very first year i.e. from the first semester or from the second semester you have to start working in the companies in the field you want to go to or actually in the field.
