Who should do a BVoc in Natural or Organic farming

  • Students who wish to make career in agriculture
  • Wants to do a start-ups in field of agriculture
  • Wants to utilize their existing land for better production
  • Those who can work at ground level in agriculture
  • Want to set up the professional farm management and scale up the business

Difference between BVoc natural/organic Farming and B.Sc Agriculture.

In B.Sc agriculture students are given theoretical knowledge by sitting in a classroom for 3 years. Maybe after completing the course when they actually work in the field they won’t know the basics of farming. Farming is purely practical. It is very difficult to learn farming sitting in a classroom.

While on the other hand students of Bvoc natural farming have spent 60% of their time on the actual farm. They gain the experience and knowledge from classroom study and practical experience by actually working on the farm.

How much demand is there After doing BVoc Natural/Organic Farming

As we know India is known for producing and exporting Organic food products. Also after Covid-19 the demand for Organic products has increased because of this rising demand the price and profits are also rising. Foreign countries give employment to people who know about organic farming.

In Foreign countries companies have tons of land to grow organic food, but there's no expert who knows about organic farming. So the demand for organic farming doesn’t only limit to India but also excess to Foreign countries.
