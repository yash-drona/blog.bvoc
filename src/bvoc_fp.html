Who should do a BVoc in Agro / Food Processing?

  • Wants to learn full process from agriculture production to process
  • Interested in marketing and distribution of agriculture products
  • Want to start their own startup
  • Already have family business in food sector
  • Belong from agriculture background and want to increase profits

Difference between BVoc in Agro/Food Processing and Bsc agriculture?

The biggest difference between BVoc and BSc is experience. In Bsc agriculture you will learn from the classroom, whereas in BVoc you will work in a food or agro processing unit. If you want to start your own startup then you will have experience about how the food and agro business operates.

When both students apply for a job in any company then BVoc students will come for an interview with 3 years of experience. There are higher chances of a BVoc student to get selected because of his experience.

If you are hard working and have relevant skills then you can also earn from 1st semester. Many BVoc students pay their day-to-day expenses themselves and some also pay their fees on their own.
