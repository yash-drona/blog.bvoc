Who should do BVoc in Healthcare?

  • Any stream student want to pursue healthcare career after 12th
  • Science students who got less marks and couldn't go to medical
  • Those who couldn't afford or don't want to go for nursing
  • Students who want to do something different than usual students
  • If you care for patient and have interest in social work

Is there any demand for BVoc healthcare after graduation?

B.VOC Health Care has the highest demand in the medical industry. After covid everyone has realized that there is a huge shortage of staff in health care. Students can get good jobs, specially after covid now the demand has started increasing in health care

Now the question is where can you go and what can you do. You can work from any small clinic to a big hospital like Jadus Stirling or Narayana Hospital. You can go and work in the hospital chains.

Government exams like UPSC, GPSC, IBPS can be given. You can also start your own health care startup. You can start your own company or your own health care related mobile apps so you can make a career in patient care management.

What are the scopes after doing Health care in BVoc?

  • From any kind of Small clinic to Hospitals
  • Government Exams like UPSC / GPSC / IBPS etc.
  • Own Healthcare Startup and Entrepreneurship
  • Patient Care Management
  • Hospital Management
  • Can work in any hospital from receptionist to Hospital manager to CEO of the Hospital
