Why is BVoc Healthcare management better than other traditional courses?

Today we are going to talk about a degree through which commerce and arts students can go into medical and health care fields. Any Commerce, Arts or Science student can do this degree called BVoc (Bachelor Of Vocation) in health care.

Let me give you the basic information about BVoc healthcare. This is also a bachelor degree like bsc nursing, but this is not a pure medical course like MBBS or BDS or BHMS, BAMS you are not going to become a doctor or a typical nurse. This is more of a health care hospital management, if you want to make your career in the medical field then this is the subject for you.

If you have done a course in hospital management, health care management, patient care management, you can make yourself from receptionist to CEO of the hospital. You can become HR Manager, Marketing Manager, Hospital Manager, General Manager in a hospital and the scope of this degree keeps increasing.

Now why should I do Bvoc rather than doing other traditional courses? That's because we provide knowledge in 40% theory and 60% practical format. You have to go to a hospital and work there to gain practical experience. You will get education in a classroom and you will get training in a company, as a student you will get the opportunity to work as an executive in the company.

Health care is a bit tricky career in that you may have to take a lot of time to develop skills in the beginning, it may happen that you don't expect a stipend for one and a half year but yes, you may start earning a stipend from the second or third year. You can pay your own fees once you start earning.

Is there any demand for BVoc healthcare after graduation?

B.VOC Health Care has the highest demand in the medical industry. After covid everyone has realized that there is a huge shortage of staff in health care. Students can get good jobs, specially after covid now the scope has started increasing in health care

Now the question is where can you go and what can you do if you can work from any small clinic to big hospital like Jadus Stirling or Narayana Hospital. You can go and work where there is a BSC list hospital chain...

Government exams like UPSC, GPSC, IBPS can be given. You can also start your own health care startup. You can start your own company or your own health care related mobile apps so you can make a career in patient care management.

What are the scopes after doing Health care in BVoc?

  • From any kind of Small clinic to Hospitals
  • Government Exams like UPSC / GPSC / IBPS etc.
  • Own Healthcare Startup and Entrepreneurship
  • Patient Care Management
  • Hospital Management
  • Can work in any hospital from receptionist to Hospital manager to CEO of the Hospital

Who should do BVoc in Healthcare?

  • Any stream student want to pursue healthcare career after 12th
  • Science students who got less marks and couldn't go to medical
  • Those who couldn't afford or don't want to go for nursing
  • Students who want to do something different than usual students
  • If you care for patient and have interest in social work
