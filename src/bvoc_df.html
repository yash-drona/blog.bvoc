What is the difference between Bachelor of Design and BVoc Design?

What is the difference between Bachelor of Design and BVoc Design? In Bachelor of Design you will study in classroom. But in BVoc Design it includes classroom study as well as you have to work on a live project in a design company or as a Intern in a designing company.

So after three years a Bachelor of Design student may be a fresher but a BVoc design student will not be a fresher, he will have three years of design experience and therefore his employability will increase or even if he wants to do a START UP then he will have the experience and knowledge to start one.
