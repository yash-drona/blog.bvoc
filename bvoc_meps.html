B.Voc in Management and entrepreneurship

What is BVoc?

Why is BVoc MEPS (Management and Entrepreneurship) better than other traditional courses?

More than 50% of students after doing such traditional degrees are unemployed or change their line and get half-time jobs. 3 crore 85 lakh students are currently studying all over India, so if you want to do something different and get a better degree in management field, then you can go for BVoc. With any business related to management or real estate or retail, you have to start working from the very first year and that's why this degree is very important because it gives you experience from the very first year.

In traditional courses like BBA, B.Com you have to study 100% theory management by sitting in the classroom all day, while in B.Voc students are studying 40% in the classroom and 60% doing some management related internships.They actually go to a company and do marketing, finance, management and also sometimes HR position. You will get management education in the classroom and actual management experience in the company.

You are a student in college and in a company you are a management executive, in college you pay a fee, you go to company and do just telly calling Even if you make calls, even if you do data entry, you will get stipend of 3000/month which means 36000 for 12 months. If you get even 3000 then you can pay your fees on your own and that is the aim of B.Voc.

Difference between BVoc MEPS( Management and entrepreneurship ) and BBA or B.COM

Now a student is doing BBA marketing and on the other hand, a student is doing BVoc marketing then what will be the difference if a BBA student learns about the market by sitting in a classroom for 3 years and a Bvoc student learning marketing in Classroom and in a company .It might become ridiculous how you can learn marketing by sitting in a classroom. If you want to learn marketing, you have to do actual marketing, you have to convince someone, you have to explain the product to someone, or if you want to do digital marketing, you have to promote a product on Facebook, Instagram and other platforms. If you don't want to learn theory and you want to take some practical experience then the student gets placed in a retail company for 3 years.

After three years, this student has done BBA Marketing, but he will be a fresher while B.Voc student will have three years of experience in marketing or banking or management, and he has also done bachelor degree now this student thinks that let me still do MBA in marketing then I will get a good job. He studies MBA for 1–2 years and will still be considered fresher on the other hand if any Bvoc student do masters in MBA or MVoc then he will have minimum 3-5 years of experience as compared to the student doing BBA.

After 5 years, this student is doing MBA again and is a freelancer. While this student is B. Voc + MBA + five years of retail management marketing experience. He has experience in banking, if these two people go to any company for the position of management, there is a post of general manager or there is a post of junior manager. Who will get the job first, the fresher or the one who has five years of experience? That is why this degree is way better for management.

We would say that management and entrepreneurship are the most in demand because you cannot manage anything without management. Moreover if in hospital, then it also needs management. If it is an education institute, it also needs management. If it is a real estate company, it also needs management.

If there is a political organization, whether you belong to BJP or Congress or Aam Aadmi Party, marketing and management finance accounting are everywhere. Whether you are in the education field or the e-commerce field, in short you will go to any organization, there will always be the demand of management people and that is the reason why you should go for a management career so in this career you have scope in any field.

If you don't want to work, you can join your existing family business and you can professionally understand how to take your family business further by pursuing a degree in management and entrepreneurship. You can start your own startup. You can give UPSC, GPSC staff selection, commission, IBPS, such government exams even if you haven’t cleared the government exam after doing B.Voc management you can make a career in sales and marketing, operations management i.e. back office or admin, human resource management, finance, banking or any other industry where the job is from a tele caller to a small receptionist to a data entry operator to a CEO.
