What are the Eligibility criteria for doing B.Voc?

B.Voc degree has been launched. Anyone can do this degree after 12th standard in Arts, Commerce, Science. Anyone can take admission in this. It is not necessary that Commerce students can do it or Science students can do it. This degree is of three years because it is a bachelor degree then three years and 6 semesters are the same as in B.B.A., B.Com, B.Sc.

Inshort the students who have passed their 12th/HSC are eligible to pursue B.Voc.and also done after diploma.
